ABOUT THE PROJECT
The project consisted in redefining the identity of the UNESCO site "Sacri Monti del Piemonte e della Lombardia" placed in northern Italy.
BRANDING

The challenge in designing the identity for the site was to include the three different aspects which made it famous: (1) the historic and religious relevance, (2) the artistic values of its art and architecture, and (3) the beauty of the landscape around it.

THE PROBLEM

To communicate the soul of the place, we looked for a naive form of the logo, with irregular borders and color. This vernacular aspect is crucial to stress the cultural role played by the local communities.

SIGNAGE & ENVIROMENTAL GRAPHICS

Being a place where it's possible to enjoy nature but also visit artistic expressions from the past, we had to integrate different activities into a single system of environmental graphics. So we designed 3 different types of signage:

THE PROBLEM

1) a big map to show the full itinerary, 2) informative graphics for each one of the chapels,
3) wayfinding signage placed throughout the path. Additionally, a small brochure has been designed to improve the visiting experience on the site.
